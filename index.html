How I see project success

Project Philosophy

Just because a project is finished, doesn't mean that it is a success. Project success requires many aspects, including:

  • A project that is on-time and on-budget
  • A viable finished product that performs as expected
  • A project that meets the clients requirements and needs
  • A happy client!
These minimum requirements should be the foundation of all development projects. Focusing on these allows a team to maximum success, while still ensuring company profit and return business.

Whether your needs include a web developer that understands requirements and can translate that into client-speak, or a project manager to ensure projects are on-time and completed as specified, allow me to help your organization meet your client's, and your organization's, expectations.
